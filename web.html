Genere


Action: Anime with intense and thrilling action sequences, often featuring combat, battles, and high-energy adventures.
Adventure: Anime that follow characters on exciting journeys, quests, or explorations in different worlds or settings.
Comedy: Anime that focus on humor, jokes, and comedic situations to entertain the audience.
Drama: Anime with emotional and intense storytelling, often dealing with complex relationships and personal struggles.
Fantasy: Anime set in magical or fantastical worlds, featuring supernatural elements, mythical creatures, and epic adventures.
